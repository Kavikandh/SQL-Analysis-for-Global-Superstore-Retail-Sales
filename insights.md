# Advanced Business Insights

## Segment Analysis
The revenue is properly distributed across Consumer, Corporate and Home Office segments.
Profit margins are stable (~11-12%), which means the pricing and cost structure are stable.

## Category Profitability
The most profitable category is technology having a margin of 13.99%.
Office Supplies is also doing well (13.69%).
Furniture has a high revenue generation but much lower margin (6.94%), which can indicate the existence of cost or discount inefficiency.

## Market Performance
APAC dominates in revenue ($3.59M), then EU, US.
The distribution of revenue in different markets shows good diversification on the global market.

## Revenue Growth Trend
The revenue levels rose progressively between 2011 and 2014 with $2.26M in 2011, and $4.30M in 2014 respectively.
This is an indicator of a stable yearly growth and good business growth.


## Discount Impact Analysis
The level of profitability reduces drastically as the level of discount goes up.
Orders that have a discount of over 20 percent often bring about negative average profit.
Very low discount rates (70-85%) have caused huge losses, which suggests intense and unsustainable pricing mechanisms.

Recommendation:
Implement stricter discount control policies and evaluate discount effectiveness across categories.

## Loss-Making Products
Several high-value products, including 3D printers and large furniture items, generate significant cumulative losses.
This implies that there could be problems in pricing, shipping expenses, or demand predictions.

Recommendation:
Re-evaluate pricing strategy and cost structure for large equipment and furniture categories.

## Regional Manager Performance
While Central region (Anna Andreadi) delivers the highest total profit, certain regions such as Southeast Asia generate relatively low profit despite high revenue.

Recommendation:
Investigate operational and pricing strategies in low-margin regions to improve profitability.

## Power BI Strategic Insights

• Revenue grew consistently from 2011–2014
• Consumer segment drives majority of sales
• Technology category yields highest margin
• Returns negatively impact profitability
• APAC is the highest revenue-generating market
